What is the primary function of Mitochondria in a eukaryotic cell?

Fig 1.1
info

Explanation (Mark Scheme)

  • B: Mitochondria are known as the "powerhouse of the cell" because they perform aerobic respiration to generate ATP, which provides energy for cellular processes.
  • A: Incorrect. Protein synthesis is primarily the role of ribosomes and the rough endoplasmic reticulum.
  • C: Incorrect. Lysosomes are responsible for waste management and digestion of cellular debris.
  • D: Incorrect. Genetic storage (DNA) and transcription occurs within the nucleus.
